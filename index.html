About The Event

Celebrate Navratri with RANGILO FUSION

Organized by Dwarka International School, RANGILO FUSION is a two-day extravaganza that brings together the vibrant colors, energetic music, and joyous dance of Dandiya in a modern avatar.

This year's event promises to be bigger and better with a perfect blend of traditional and contemporary elements. Whether you're a Dandiya pro or a first-timer, there's something for everyone!

Date: September 27-28, 2025
Time: 6:00 PM onwards
Venue: Dwarka International School
